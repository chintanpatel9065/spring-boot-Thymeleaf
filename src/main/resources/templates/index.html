Manage Priority

Define and control the urgency and importance of tasks across your organization with the Priority Management module. Effortlessly create new priority levels, edit existing ones, view all defined priorities, and delete unused or outdated levels as needed.

Each task can be assigned a specific priority to ensure proper attention and timely execution. This helps teams focus on what matters most and keeps projects moving forward efficiently.

By maintaining a clear hierarchy of task urgency—such as High, Medium, Very Low, Critical, or Low—your team can stay aligned, avoid bottlenecks, and meet critical deadlines with confidence.Keep your workflows agile and optimized with this intuitive priority management interface.

Manage Status

Track and define the life cycle of tasks within your organization using the Status Management module. Easily create new status labels, update existing statuses, view all available status options, and remove outdated ones with just a few clicks.

Each task is linked to a status that reflects its current progress—such as To Do, In Progress, Under Review, or Completed. This ensures transparency, improves coordination, and keeps everyone informed of task movement across projects.

With a well-defined set of statuses, your teams can streamline workflows, identify bottlenecks early, and deliver consistent results. Manage Projects

Keep your projects organized and under control with this powerful Project Management interface. Easily create new projects, edit existing project details, view all current projects, and delete outdated ones with just a few clicks.

Each project acts as a container for multiple tasks, allowing you to monitor the full scope, timeline, and deliverables. Stay on top of your team’s progress by tracking the status of associated tasks and their comments within each project.

Whether you're overseeing large-scale initiatives or smaller internal goals, this intuitive CRUD dashboard ensures your project structure remains clean, organized, and aligned with your organizational workflow.

Manage Tasks

Take full control of your organizational workflow with our Task Management module. Effortlessly create new tasks, update task details, view all assigned tasks, and remove completed or obsolete tasks with ease.

Each task can have multiple comments to help track discussions, updates, and changes in real-time. This ensures better collaboration, accountability, and traceability throughout the task’s lifecycle.

Stay focused and organized by maintaining clarity on task priorities, progress, and responsible team members. This user-friendly CRUD interface helps streamline task execution and aligns seamlessly with project goals.
